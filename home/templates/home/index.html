How it works!

1

Bill needs a picture for his blog, he describes the shot on the request form.

2

Photographers from all over the world view his request and if its convenient, grab the shot Bill needs.

3

The photographer submits the photo to Crowdphoto.org for Bill's approval.

4

Bill is alerted that his photo is available. He approves the photo and high-fives the photographer.
